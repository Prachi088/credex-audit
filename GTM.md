Submit "Show HN: Free AI spend auditor for startups". HN rewards tools that are genuinely useful and free. A good Show HN can drive 500-2000 visitors in 24 hours.

Post a thread: "Most startups are overpaying for AI tools. Here's what I found after auditing 50 audits from our free tool [link to shareable audit]." Tag relevant founders and indie hackers.

DM 20 founders on X/Twitter who have tweeted about AI tool costs. Not a pitch — offer to run a free audit for their stack. Each one who shares their result is distribution.
