## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Average deal assumption:
- A startup spending $500/mo on AI tools → potential Credex purchase of $2,000-5,000 in credits upfront
- Credex margin assumption: 15-25% on resold credits
- Revenue per converted customer: $300-1,250 (one-time) + repeat purchases

**Conservative estimate: $500 LTV per converted customer**
**Optimistic estimate: $2,000 LTV per converted customer**
